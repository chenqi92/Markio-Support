Markio 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 5 月 18 日

Markio 是一款本地优先的 Markdown 阅读与写作应用。你的笔记、工作区、历史快照、本地搜索索引和应用偏好设置会保存在你的设备或你选择的文件夹中。

Markio 不收集的数据

Markio 不运行广告、跟踪、遥测或分析服务。Markio 不提供 Markio 云账号，开发者不会接收你的笔记、文件、API Key、使用历史或搜索索引。

保存在设备上的数据

Markio 会在你的设备上保存应用设置、最近打开的工作区、界面状态、文档历史快照、回收站元数据和本地知识库索引。API Key 和服务凭据会尽可能保存在操作系统钥匙串中。

可选第三方服务

Markio 包含一些可选集成，只有在你配置或主动使用时才会运行：

  • OpenAI、Anthropic、Google Gemini、DeepSeek、本地 Ollama 或自定义 OpenAI 兼容端点等 AI 服务。
  • WebDAV、S3 兼容存储、Dropbox、Google Drive 或 Git 远端等用户选择的同步 / 存储服务。
  • PicGo、微信公众号相关工具等可选图片上传或发布流程。

当你使用这些集成时，你选择发送的内容以及对应服务需要的凭据会直接传输到你选择的第三方服务，并受该第三方服务的条款和隐私政策约束。Markio 开发者不会接收或存储这些数据。

网络访问

Markio 只会为你启用的功能使用网络访问，例如 AI 请求、同步 / 存储操作、更新检查、OAuth 流程或图片上传。Markio 不会将网络访问用于广告或跟踪。

你的控制权

如果不配置网络集成，你可以完全以本地方式使用 Markio。你可以在 Markio 设置中移除 API Key 或断开服务，也可以通过 macOS 标准存储位置删除本地应用数据。

Markio Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Markio is a local-first Markdown reader and writing app. Your notes, workspaces, history snapshots, local search indexes, and app preferences are stored on your device or in folders you choose.

Data Markio Does Not Collect

Markio does not run advertising, tracking, telemetry, or analytics services. Markio does not operate a Markio cloud account, and the developer does not receive your notes, files, API keys, usage history, or search index.

Data Stored On Your Device

Markio stores app settings, recently opened workspaces, UI state, document history snapshots, trash metadata, and local knowledge-base indexes on your device. API keys and service credentials are stored in the operating system keychain when possible.

Optional Third-Party Services

Markio includes optional integrations that only run after you configure or invoke them:

  • AI providers such as OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, DeepSeek, local Ollama, or a custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint.
  • User-selected sync or storage providers such as WebDAV, S3-compatible storage, Dropbox, Google Drive, or Git remotes.
  • Optional image upload or publishing workflows such as PicGo or WeChat-related tools.

When you use these integrations, the content you choose to send, plus the credentials needed for that provider, are transmitted directly to the selected third-party service according to that service's terms and privacy policy. The Markio developer does not receive or store that data.

Network Access

Markio uses network access only for features you enable, such as AI requests, sync / storage operations, update checks, OAuth flows, or image upload. Markio does not use network access for advertising or tracking.

Your Controls

You can keep Markio fully local by not configuring network integrations. You can remove API keys or disconnect providers from Markio settings, and you can delete local app data from your device using standard macOS storage locations.

Contact
